I offer a few different services related to development. Meetings can be conducted through Discord, Google Meet, Skype, or another platform if you prefer.

Freelance App Development

Do you need a full webpage or app built for you? I am more than happy to provide that service. Please note that pricing varies depending on the size and scope of the application you need built, as well as extra services such as hosting.

Pair Programming Sessions

We can spend some time working on one of your projects together. With this service, I will work with you directly in a 1-on-1 environment to write code for your project together. Unlike other services, I will be directly writing code with you and contributing to your project.

Code Review

If you are stuck on a certain bug, or aren't sure if your code is the most readable or maintainable, this is the service for you! This is a 1-on-1 session where I will walk through your code with you, identify potential concerns, and help resolve issues. While I will not be writing code for you, I will gladly talk you through some potential approaches.

Mentoring Session

If you need some guidance on your development journey, whether it's determining the next steps of your learning path, seeking advice on your job search, or general coding questions, this is your chance. This service is a 1-on-1 meeting to talk through any questions or concerns you are having.
